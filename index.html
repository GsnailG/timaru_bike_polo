TIMARU BIKE POLO

Timaru Bike Polo started in 2011 with just a small handful of people. We have hosted a few tournaments, including the austalisia's in 2017, as well as a yearly tournament called Winter Whack. We welcone everyone to come down, have a watch, or even join in!
